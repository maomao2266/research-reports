Theoretical Framework

Catalyst analysis answers Michael Steinhardt's fourth question: "When and how will the situation change?"

Following Sonkin & Johnson (2017): "A catalyst is any event that begins to close the gap between a stock's price and your estimate of its intrinsic value." A catalyst acts as a "color-blindness vaccine" — it makes the market see what your variant perception already sees.

What Consensus Misses — The One Insight

The market models ASML as a cyclical semiconductor equipment company — revenue tied to wafer fab equipment (WFE) cycles. But ASML is becoming a compound growth royalty on compute complexity. Every new transistor node requires more EUV passes (N3: 20 layers → N2: 25+ layers → A14: 30+ layers). Every additional logic layer, every DRAM shrink to EUV, every High-NA transition — these are structural demand drivers, not cyclical. The 2026 CMD will be the moment this transforms from "bull thesis" to "consensus."
